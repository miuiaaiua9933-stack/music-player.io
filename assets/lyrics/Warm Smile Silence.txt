I was still nobody then
Searching hard for every answer I could find
Even learning how to smile
Started feeling harder every night

Then you came and said nothing at all
With the same warm smile you always wore
Some kinds of kindness reach before the words
Maybe that is what hope is for

You were seeing a view a hundred steps ahead
Your back became the road that guided me instead
Even when I couldn’t catch your light
You taught my darkest mornings how to rise

You picked up a heart about to break
Held me steady so I still could walk again
Meeting you has been the gift that proves
My life was blessed to know your name

Smiling for the sake of someone else
Looks so easy, but it’s hard to do
Yet beside that kind of gentleness
I could always see the truth in you

Years were carrying hidden pain
Stories never spoken out loud
Still you showed me how to face the wind
Standing strong and standing proud

I am still far, still unfinished now
With so little I can give to you
But with every light you placed in me
I will shine for someone new

If I lose my way someday again
If another silent night appears
All the words you left inside my heart
Will lead me through the dark with no fear

You were seeing a view a hundred steps ahead
Even now your light is with me where I stand
Though the years may pass, inside my chest
You still quietly light the road ahead

Thank you is too small a phrase to say
For the gratitude I carry every day
Meeting you remains the miracle
And the pride of all my life today
